About

Orin (aka Sleepmode)

I’m an Australian video game enthusiast with a particular love of fighting games and arcade-style tests of skill. This place is usually where you can find me writing about that, and sometimes other things. Older writings can be found on my Wordpress Blog, which is no longer in use because I hate QOL features being locked behind a paywall.
Links to my Twitter, Twitch and Youtube channels can all be found below, as well as on my links page.

Fighting Games

I like to think of myself as a “fighting game knowledge seeker” – I love collecting lots of abstract knowledge about fighting games and relaying that knowledge to anyone with ears to hear it.

You can often find me playing, commentating, or otherwise just being interested in:
Guilty Gear XX ACPR: Venom
Advanced V.G.2: Chiho, Kyoko
The King of Fighters XI: Oswald/Vanessa/K'

That said, never underestimate my limitless genre curiosity, or my extremely limited self-control. Here are a handful of other games that I’m almost always in the mood to play:
Capcom vs SNK 2: C-Cammy/Kim/Chun-Li
DNF Duel: Ghostblade
SoulCalibur: Hwang
Vampire Savior: Demitri
Virtua Fighter series: Sarah, Akira

Other Games I’m Into

I’m into lots of things, but the main games I like to play outside of fighting games are arcade racing games, light gun shooters and occasionally rhythm games. I also have a speedrun I need to actually work on.

Racing Games: I really only play arcade racing games, because I am eternally resentful of simulators and the constant desire for realistic physics and handling over going fast and looking really cool. I’m a huge fan of OutRun 2SP and PSX-era Ridge Racer, though I am also contractually obligated to play a fair bit of Wangan Midnight Maximum Tune.

Light Gun Shooters: My main focus is the PS2 version of Time Crisis II, though you can also find me playing other games in the Time Crisis series, as well as Point Blank and even Vampire Night on occasion.

Rhythm Games: My main game is DanceRush Stardom (find my premium sessions on my side channel!), but I also play Sound Voltex and Chunithm from time to time.

Speedruns: I’m currently developing a speedrun for a dinky action-platformer called Power Rangers Super Legends. Check out the SRC leaderboard here.

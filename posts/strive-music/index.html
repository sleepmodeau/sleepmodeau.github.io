Musing About Guilty Gear Strive's Music

Remember those jokes people made about Guilty Gear’s music? The ones that were like, “Oh hey check it out, Daisuke Ishiwatari included a free video game with his latest heavy metal album!” I mean, fair enough people make those jokes. After all, the series has some absolutely iconic, expertly composed character themes that sell the player on the pace of the fight they’re conducting and the characters who act out that fight.1

  1. I’m not even kidding when I say that fighting Justice is only as bearable as it is for me because her GGXX theme, “Meet Again”, is an absolute god damn masterpiece. ↩︎
